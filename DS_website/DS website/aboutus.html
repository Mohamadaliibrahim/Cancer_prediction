About HealthDx

Revolutionizing cancer detection through advanced AI and machine learning technologies

Our Mission

At HealthDx, we're transforming cancer diagnosis by developing cutting-edge machine learning models that analyze medical data with unprecedented accuracy. Our platform empowers healthcare professionals with AI-driven insights to detect cancer at its earliest, most treatable stages.

Founded in 2020 by a team of oncologists and data scientists, HealthDx combines medical expertise with artificial intelligence to create diagnostic tools that improve patient outcomes and reduce healthcare costs.

Our Technology

Our proprietary AI system analyzes multiple data points including medical imaging, genetic markers, and patient history to predict cancer probability with over 95% accuracy in clinical trials.

Deep Learning Models

Our neural networks are trained on millions of anonymized patient records to recognize subtle patterns indicative of early-stage cancers.

Predictive Analytics

Advanced algorithms assess risk factors and biomarkers to provide personalized cancer risk assessments.

Clinical Decision Support

Real-time analysis helps physicians make more informed diagnostic and treatment decisions.

Our Impact

HealthDx is currently deployed in over 50 medical institutions worldwide, helping to:

  • Reduce false negatives by 40% compared to traditional screening methods
  • Detect cancers an average of 18 months earlier than standard protocols
  • Decrease unnecessary invasive procedures by 35% through more accurate risk stratification

Our technology is particularly effective in detecting breast, lung, and colorectal cancers, with expansion to additional cancer types underway.
